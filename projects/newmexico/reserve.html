Reserve NM can easily be described as a hidden gem. Tucked away in the Reserve mountains the small town is one of the only ones nearby that is located by running water. A population of 314 puts it as one of the bigger towns on our list. The town of Reserve has a school a store andr a mail center. Reserve is also where the police station is located for Catron County.
