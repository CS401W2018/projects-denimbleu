Fashion Throughout Time

The world always has prioritized fashion and loved new designs all throughout history. Some might wonder why there is such praise to these designers. The truth that I see is that not only do these designers make clothing but that they also make art. Their designs and clothing are pieces of art. Even the runway shows are just a show of their new artwork. In past history new designs were loved by the people and praised, times have not changed. We can see that our world is often shaped by the clothes we choose to wear, which is why these designers have and still are shaping the world we live in today.
