An Absolute link will always work, its considered easy. Relative links, link to your website only. They are "related to us"

Three rules to remember is 1. period means what folder you are in/the current folder 2. two periods means parent folder or to go up a folder 3. the / means start at the root.

Links do not always need to be in the nav but if they are anywhere else they will not show up when the page changes
